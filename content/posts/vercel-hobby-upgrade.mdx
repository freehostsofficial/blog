Vercel announced a major update to its free Hobby plan in January 2026, bringing substantial increases to usage limits and several new features that were previously only available on paid tiers.

The changes take effect immediately for all existing and new users, making Vercel's free tier even more compelling for developers building personal projects and small applications.

## What Changed

### Build Minutes

The most significant change is the increase in build minutes. The free Hobby plan now includes **6,000 build minutes per month**, up from the previous 600. This is a 10x increase that accommodates larger projects and more frequent deployments.

For context, a typical Next.js site with 100 pages takes about 45 seconds to build. At the previous limit of 600 minutes, you could build about 800 times per month. With the new limit, that jumps to over 8,000 builds.

### Serverless Function Execution

Vercel has also increased the serverless function execution limit from 100 GB-hours to **500 GB-hours per month** on the Hobby plan. This is particularly impactful for projects using API routes, middleware, or Edge Functions.

### New Deployment Regions

The Hobby plan now supports deployments in **three additional regions**: Sydney, Mumbai, and São Paulo. Previously, free plan users were limited to Washington D.C., Frankfurt, and Singapore.

### Community Analytics

A new **Community Analytics** feature is now available on all plans, including Hobby. It provides aggregated, anonymized performance data that helps developers understand how their sites perform across different regions and devices.

## What Stayed the Same

Some limits remain unchanged to maintain the distinction between free and paid plans:

- **Bandwidth:** 100 GB per month (unchanged)
- **Team seats:** 1 (unchanged)
- **Concurrent builds:** 1 (unchanged)
- **Preview deployment retention:** 14 days (unchanged)

## Comparison with Other Platforms

Here's how Vercel's updated free tier compares to other major hosting platforms:

| Feature | Vercel (Hobby) | Netlify (Free) | Cloudflare Pages (Free) |
|---|---|---|---|
| Build minutes | 6,000/mo | 300/mo | 500/mo |
| Bandwidth | 100 GB | 100 GB | Unlimited |
| Serverless functions | 500 GB-hr | 125k requests | 100k requests/day |
| Custom domains | Yes | Yes | Yes |

## What This Means for Developers

The upgrades make Vercel's free tier particularly attractive for:

- **Next.js developers** who need more build time for larger applications
- **API-heavy projects** that rely on serverless functions
- **Global audiences** that benefit from additional edge regions
- **Budget-conscious teams** who want to test and prototype before upgrading

## How to Access the New Limits

The upgraded limits are applied automatically to all Hobby plan users. No action is required on your part. You can verify your current usage and limits in the Vercel dashboard under **Settings > Usage**.

## Looking Ahead

Industry analysts see this move as part of a broader trend of hosting platforms expanding their free tiers to attract developers early in their projects. As competition intensifies, we can expect even more generous offerings throughout 2026.
